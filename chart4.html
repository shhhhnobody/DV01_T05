Notes:

Scope & Time Series:

  • The Detection Method filter can switch between 'Camera Issued', 'Police Issued' and 'Other' fines/offences.
  • Police-issued fines/offences data has been consistently collected since 2017 in the dataset.

Jurisdictional Differences:

  • NSW: Excludes some red-light/speed offences before 2019; speed bands changed in 2009.
  • QLD: Only counts detections confirmed by the Traffic Camera Office.
  • TAS: Had equipment and software issues affecting detections in several years.
  • WA: Detection modes include On-The-Spot (OTS), mobile, fixed, and average-speed cameras.

Data Limitations:

  • Missing data: ACT (2022) is not available, and low volumes are seen for Victoria (2024, due to Protected Industrial Action).
  • Data is updated by each jurisdiction and may be revised over time.
