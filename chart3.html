Notes:

Scope & Time Series:

  • From 2023, more detailed data is collected (e.g., location, age group, arrests, charges). Earlier years will show blanks or zeros for these fields.

Jurisdictional Differences:

  • NSW: Excludes some red-light/speed offences before 2019; speed bands changed in 2009.
  • QLD: Only counts detections confirmed by the Traffic Camera Office.
  • TAS: Had equipment and software issues affecting detections in several years.
  • WA: Detection modes include On-The-Spot (OTS), mobile, fixed, and average-speed cameras.

Unknown Values:

  • Location:“Unknown” indicates fines/arrests/charges where the location was not recorded.

Data Limitations:

  • Missing data: ACT (2022) is not available, and low volumes are seen for Victoria (2024, due to Protected Industrial Action).
  • Incomplete Location Reporting: NT, QLD, TAS, and WA did not record fine/arrests/charges counts across different locations. ACT only recorded fine/arrests/charges counts for major Australian cities. Some ACT records lack explicit location and age-group labels and are grouped under “Unknown”.
  • Jurisdiction Coverage: All jurisdictions have records for fines. Arrests data is missing for NSW, QLD, TAS, VIC, and WA. Charges data is missing for QLD, VIC, and WA.
  • Data is updated by each jurisdiction and may be revised over time.

Interactivity:

  • Tooltip (Hover): Hovering the mouse over the main chart area will display a dynamic a tooltip box. The tooltip shows the exact count for the selected Enforcement Type (Fines, Arrests, or Charges).
  • Highlight (Hover): Hovering the mouse over the main chart area will highlight the corresponding location across the bar chart and choropleth map, ensuring linked visual feedback.
